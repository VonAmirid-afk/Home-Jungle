About us

Welcome to Home Jungle, a distinguished flower farm located in the tranquil village of Kaloyanovo, in the Plovdiv region of Bulgaria. Renowned for our unwavering commitment to excellence, we specialize in cultivating premium-quality greenhouse flowers. By combining advanced horticultural techniques with the rich agricultural traditions of the region, we take pride in producing blooms of exceptional beauty and freshness. At Home Jungle, we aim to deliver the finest floral creations, ensuring every arrangement reflects elegance and sophistication.
